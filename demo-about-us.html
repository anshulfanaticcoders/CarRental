Our Story

From a simple idea to thousands of happy journeys.

Vrooem was born from a simple frustration: renting a car shouldn't feel like navigating an obstacle course of hidden fees, confusing insurance, and impersonal service. We set out to build something better.

Founded in 2022 in Amsterdam, we've grown from a local startup into a platform trusted by thousands of travellers across Europe — combining travel expertise with cutting-edge technology.
