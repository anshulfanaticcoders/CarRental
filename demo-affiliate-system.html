Vrooem Partner Agreement

By registering as a Vrooem Partner, you agree to the following terms:

1. Commission Structure
You will receive a commission on each eligible booking made through your unique QR codes.

2. QR Code Usage
QR codes must be displayed only at registered locations. Codes are geo-restricted.

3. Payouts
Commissions are paid monthly via bank transfer.

4. Termination
Either party may terminate with 30 days notice.
